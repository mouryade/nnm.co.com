DETAIL INFO

Why Choose Us

Nataraj Nritya Mandir (N.N.M) was established in 1990 by Sankari De. This a great platform for learning all types of dance. Here an annual examination is arranged by "NNM" taken by "Purbio Sangeet Parisad & Kala Kandro" for verifying students skills and after exam they will get a certificate and result. From class PP-1 to 7Year in each and every types of dance like Rabindra Nritya, Kathak, Bharatnatyam, etc.

Rabindranritya

The songs of Rabindranath and dances on those together have become an essential element of Bengali daily life for decades.

Kathak

Kathak is one of the main genres of ancient Indian classical dance and is traditionally regarded to have originated from the travelling bards of North India referred as Kathakars or storytellers.

Bharatanatyam

Bharatanatyam, a pre-eminent Indian classical dance form presumably the oldest classical dance heritage of India is regarded as mother of many other Indian classical dance forms.

Folk Dance

We all know that Bengali is famous all over the world for its culture. In the culture of West Bengal, you will find a combination of different diversity and richness. At present, the folk dance of West Bengal has also attracted the attention of the people of the world. West Bengal folk dance is world-famous for its richness and taste. Every district of West Bengal is famous for its dance and culture. Even foreigners like to take part in such dances.

Adhuik Dance

Adhunik is a Bengali word which means modern. In India, the word Adhunik is used to describe anythings that is modern or up-to-date. 'Adhunik Dance'(Modern Dance) is modern type of dance which is one of a popular dance in West Bengal. Students get scope to learn and perform different types of Adhunik Dances.
